KINETIC GUARDIAN

GAME DESCRIPTION

School of kinetic magic is under attack! Defend your castle from monsters that attack from three different lanes. Gather kinetic crystals by walking in real life and use them to buy and upgrade new spells. You must defend the castle at any cost. Switch lanes to counter monster attacks. Select your arsenal from huge variety of different skills. Use gold and kinetic crystals to upgrade your character's stats and skills!
